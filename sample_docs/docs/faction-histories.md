## Introduction

This document compiles the extended histories, internal communiqués, and primary source documents for all three factions in Void Protocol. Unlike the World Lore Compendium (VP-LORE-001), which presents events from a neutral narrative perspective, this document preserves the viewpoint and voice of each faction. Contradictions between faction accounts are intentional — they reflect genuine disagreements about what happened.

## Part I: Voidborn Conclave

### 1.1 Founding Documents

**Excerpt from Dr. Lin Chen's Founding Manifesto — "The Voices in the Static" (2318)**

> We stand at the most significant moment in human history, and we are responding with rifles.
>
> The entities that emerged from the Kedraxis Void Rift on 2301-06-15 are not predators. They are not wildlife. The Terran Compact's Void Response Command has classified them as "hostile fauna" in seventeen separate official communications, and not one of those communications has engaged with the most obvious fact about the entities' behavior during the Unraveling: they did not immediately attack.
>
> Technician Petra Solis documented eight Voidborn entities surrounding the Kesslerium processing bay at K7-III-07 for four minutes and seventeen seconds before facility security initiated an armed response. The entities' first aggressive behavior occurred after the security response, not before it. This is in the official record. Void Response Command has not retracted or amended the classification.
>
> I am a scientist. I am founding the Voidborn Conclave because science requires that we look at evidence before we form conclusions. The evidence says: we were not attacked first.
>
> The resonance signature of Void entities — consistently measured at 0.8847 Hz across all documented specimens — is not a combat signal. It is a carrier frequency. These entities are broadcasting. The Terran Compact's response has been to shoot the radio.
>
> I do not know what they are saying. I intend to find out. I expect it will take my entire life. I hope I am wrong about how long it will take.
>
> — Dr. Lin Chen, 2318-03-12

### 1.2 The Conclave's Research Archive Summary

The Voidborn Conclave has maintained continuous research operations since 2318, initially from a provisional facility in Kedraxis-7's Serethal continent and since 2341 from the Communion Spire. Below is a condensed summary of research milestones:

**2318 — Founding and First Systematic Studies**
Dr. Lin Chen establishes the standardized measurement protocol for Void entity observation. Key finding: all Void entities vocalize at 0.8847 Hz regardless of type, size, or behavioral state. Chen's measurement protocol remains the standard used by all researchers, including Iron Sovereignty intelligence, as of 2387.

**2327 — VE-Type Classification System**
Dr. Chen and colleague Dr. Yuri Okafor propose the VE-Type classification (I through IV, with V theoretical). The classification is based on observed intelligence markers: Type I shows reflex-only behavior; Type II shows tool use (observed: a Warden entity using a shard of hull plating as a weapon, apparently by choice); Type III shows strategic coordination; Type IV (Architect class) demonstrates behaviors consistent with individual identity and long-term memory.

**2331 — The Communion Spire Discovery**
A Conclave expedition to the Northern Ruin discovers the structure later designated the Communion Spire — an existing structure within which a VE-Type IV entity has apparently been residing for an unknown period. The Spire shows signs of modification: the interior resonance characteristics appear deliberately shaped to maintain a stable 0.8847 Hz field. Dr. Chen's field notes: "Either this structure was built by the entity, or the entity chose to inhabit a structure that matched its natural resonance. Neither conclusion is simple."

**2341 — Relocation to New Amora Station**
Following the station's completion, the Conclave establishes a permanent embassy and research partnership with station administration. The proximity to the Communion Spire allows continuous observation. Architect Thessa Vorin's design, which maintains station-wide VRF at 0.8847, is noted with interest by Conclave researchers: "The station resonates at the same frequency as a Void entity vocalization. Whether Vorin intended this as communication or fortification is not documented. She has not responded to our inquiries on this point." — Conclave Research Log, 2341-09-14.

**2349 — Dr. Lin Chen's Death**
Dr. Chen dies on 2349-07-02 at age 91. She has not achieved communication with a Void entity. Her final research note: "I have spent 31 years listening. The signal is consistent. The message, if there is one, exceeds my remaining time. I am naming my successor Cassian Rhae, who is 33 years old and has my stubbornness without my impatience. He may succeed where I could not. I leave him everything I know, which amounts to: they broadcast at 0.8847 Hz, they are not mindlessly hostile, and we are not listening carefully enough."

**2351 — Elder Rhae's First Architect Observation**
Cassian Rhae (age 35) achieves first meaningful contact with the VE-Type IV in the Communion Spire apex chamber. His audio log from 2351-09-12 (now the collectible item "Rhae's Codex") records: "It turned to face me. It did not attack. It resonated. I have 36 more years to find out what this means."

**2367 — The Harmony Hypothesis**
Rhae publishes internally the "Harmony Hypothesis": that the universal VRF constant of 0.8847 is not merely a physical measurement but a frequency that appears in multiple unrelated contexts because Ω-Space and normal space share a fundamental harmonic resonance. The hypothesis predicts that any structure designed to emit 0.8847 Hz would be "legible" to Void entities as a communication attempt. New Amora Station, if Rhae is correct, has been "speaking" to Ω-Space since 2341.

**2374 — The Theta-9 Designation**
The Communion Spire's VE-Type IV entity, now continuously observed for 23 years, is formally designated "Elder Warform Theta-9" in Conclave records. "Theta" (the Greek letter) references Dr. Lin Chen's classification convention; "9" is the 9th distinct behavioral pattern catalogued for this entity; "Elder Warform" reflects the dual-category classification (Warform as its physical combat capability class; Elder as its apparent age relative to other Architects). Rhae objects to "Warform" in the name: "It has never once initiated combat with a Conclave researcher in 23 years of continuous observation. 'Warform' describes capability, not character. I will continue to object to this name indefinitely." The name stands.

**2381 — The Chamber Incident**
Three Fractured operatives breach the Resonance Chamber attempting to destroy the VRF stabilizer. Thessa Vorin, 84, is killed in a corridor explosion. The Conclave issues a formal statement condemning the attack but notably refuses to join the Iron Sovereignty's call for Fractured suppression operations. Rhae's personal note (not part of the official statement): "Vorin understood something that none of us have fully understood yet. Destroying her work would have been a tragedy. Destroying her was a tragedy. These are not the same magnitude of tragedy, but I find myself angrier about the second."

## Part III: The Fractured

### 3.1 The Fractured — No Founding Document

The Fractured have no founding document because they have no founding event. The faction emerged organically from 2335 onward, coalescing around shared disillusionment rather than a specific ideology. The first use of the name appears in an Iron Sovereignty border patrol log dated 2335-03-09:

> "Patrol report, Northern Ruin perimeter. Unauthorized settlement observed at grid reference NR-0337. Approximately 47 occupants. Settlement occupants declined to identify faction affiliation. When asked how they identified themselves, one occupant stated 'We are fractured.' Settlement deemed non-threatening. No action taken. — Cpl. Daiko Wen, 2335-03-09."

*(Note: The same Cpl. Daiko Wen died at the Volhari Outpost Incident 37 years later in 2372.)*

## Part IV: New Amora Station — Administrative Records

### 4.1 Vorin's Construction Notes — Partial Recovery

*The following notes were recovered from Thessa Vorin's personal storage after her death in 2381. They were not part of her official engineering documentation. Chief Engineer Malo Fenn has been unable to determine where they were stored or why they were not archived with her primary records.*

**Entry 1 — 2334-11-22 (Construction Start)**

"First day on site. The commission from the Terran Compact is clear: neutral ground in the Shattered Belt, accessible to all parties, resistant to faction conflict. The technical specifications are mine to determine.

I have chosen a hexagonal ring structure. The mathematics are elegant: six primary rings, 18.5 meters in diameter each, arranged in a hexagonal pattern creates a resonance harmonic at the ring junctions of precisely 0.8847 Hz. This is not coincidence — I calculated it deliberately. I want the station to feel... settled. Calm. The 0.8847 Hz resonance is not aggressive; it's the same frequency that Void entities emit when they are dormant. A station that breathes at that frequency is a station that tells the entities: this place is not a threat.

Whether this will work, I don't know. I am an architect, not a xenobiologist. But Dr. Chen's research is compelling, and she has agreed that the design hypothesis is worth testing.

I have not documented this rationale in the official engineering submission. The Terran Compact commission would not understand it, and I prefer they fund the construction without objection."

**Entry 7 — 2337-03-14**

"The equations in the resonance coupling system are giving me trouble. The standard engineering formulas produce a 0.8851 Hz harmonic at the ring junctions — close but wrong. I have been working on a modified approach for three months.

Dr. Chen visited last week. She asked what frequency I was targeting. When I told her 0.8847, she looked at me for a very long time, then said: 'Who told you that number?'

I told her I derived it from the Void entity vocalizations she published in 2318. She said: 'That's not where I got it.'

I asked her where she got it.

She said: 'I measured it. At K7-III-07, 2301-06-15. From the facility's resonance sensors. But I didn't publish where the measurement originated — I published it as a derived theoretical value. Fewer questions that way.'

I asked her why fewer questions was preferable.

She said: 'Because the sensors at K7-III-07 detected the 0.8847 Hz resonance for approximately 18 hours before the Rift opened. Not after. Before.'

We sat with that for a while."

**Entry 19 — 2340-08-12 (Pre-Completion)**

"The equations are finalized. Forty-seven equations total, building a cascade of dependencies. Engineer Fenn (junior, then) reviewed the first 31 and pronounced them sound. The remaining 16 I have documented but not explained — they would require a level of context that I cannot provide in a standard engineering document.

I am not hiding anything. I am simply aware that some truths are too large for the documents we have. If someone needs to understand equations 32 through 47, they will need to ask me directly.

Or, if I am gone: they will need to find the annotations I have left in the structure itself.

Every major junction node in this station has a housing inscription. Not decorative. Mathematical.
